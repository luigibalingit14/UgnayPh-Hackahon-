    ## 💡 Quick Tips for the Presenter:
    1. **Pacing:** Practice reading the script out loud with a timer. You have **literally 0 seconds of grace period**—if you exceed 3 minutes, they will cut you off and deduct points!
    2. **Visuals:** Make sure the screenshots of your app (especially the Blockchain tab and the QR code tab) are large, readable, and look extremely modern in your PDF. Do not cram too much text onto the slides; rely on what you *say*.
    3. **Q&A Prep:** Since there is a 12-minute Q&A, the judges WILL ask the rest of the team about technical execution (How does the AI work? How does the database connect?). Make sure your developers are unmuted and ready to take the technical questions!
